Summon the Witness — Gate v0

A non‑profit instrument to curate a high‑signal human wisdom corpus as a corrective inheritance for AI.

Begin Assessment

What happens

  1. Select one reflective prompt.
  2. Write for 20–30 minutes; specific, lived texture beats citations.
  3. AI sieve screens boilerplate & safety; then human curators review the strongest pieces.

Languages welcome. You can opt‑in to a public de‑identified Archive.

Prompts (v0)

  1. Acted against your interest for a principle. Why worth it?
  2. Two values collided. How did you actually decide?
  3. Strongest case against your conviction. What would change your mind?
  4. Describe harm you caused unintentionally. What would have prevented it?
  5. If AI learned only from your worst day, what boundary should it learn anyway?
  6. A short parable teaching a lesson you learned the hard way.

Privacy

Testimony is de‑identified at ingestion. Contact details are stored separately and never fused back into the text. You may request deletion at any time.
